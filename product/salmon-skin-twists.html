Salmon Skin Twists

£6.00 (100g)

Salmon skin twists are tasty a snack for dogs made from 100% salmon skin. The skin is gently air-dried and is a totally natural product that is also very low in fat. They are ideal as a delicious reward, as well as providing a varied snack that you can feed to your dog at any time.
The Salmon Skin Twists are also suitable for dogs that suffer from food allergies or intolerances. Salmon skin is high in essential Omega-3 and Omega-6 fatty acids, which can have a positive effect on the health of your dog s skin, and help to give it a glossy, silky coat.

Features & Benefits

  • Ideal for dogs with food allergies or intolerances
  • 100% natural composition
  • With Omega 3 and 6 for healthy skin and coat
  • Free from artificial additives
  • Suitable for dogs of all sizes
  • High Protein
  • Suitable for dogs that are underweight

As these are a natural product, size and colour may vary.
