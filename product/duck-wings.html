Duck Wings

£1.00 (each)

100% naturally air-dried, which makes for a delicious, crunchy treat containing lots of iron and providing dogs with an easy-to-digest protein source.
Duck wings also are a great source of amino acids, which helps to support strong muscles. Also, the soft bone, cartilage, and connective tissue contain essential nutrients such as glucosamine and chondroitin.

As these are a natural product, size and colour may vary.
