Venison Skin

£3.00 (100g)

Venison has less protein than beef, but it contains less fat and cholesterol, making it increasingly popular for dog treats.
Venison is a good source of B vitamins and minerals such as zinc, phosphorous, and iron. It helps dogs maintain healthy energy levels and many dogs enjoy the taste.
Pet treats containing venison are a good option for dogs who may have food sensitivities or allergies to other protein sources. Since venison is a novel protein, it may help reduce allergens and skin irritations in pets with food-related issues. Venison skin is hypoallergenic making it great for dogs with allergies.
