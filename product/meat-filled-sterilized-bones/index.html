Meat Filled Sterilized Bones

£2.00 (each)

Our meat filled sterilised bones are a classic dog treat made from UK sourced British beef marrowbone and stuffed with beef meat derivatives.
These long-lasting treats provide mental stimulation, support muscle development and have great benefit for healthy gums and teeth
They are a great rawhide alternative.

Features & Benefits

  • Grain and Gluten Free
  • Gently air dried
  • Dental Aid
  • Free from artificial additives
  • Suitable for dogs of all sizes
  • High Protein
  • Low Fat

As these are a natural product, size and colour may vary.
